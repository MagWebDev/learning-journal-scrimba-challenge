Hi! My name is Magda. I've been learning to code for almost 3 years. Join me in my journey!

My path to become a frontend developer isn't easy. From the beginning I've been learning everything by myself. I do not have any technical backround. I've started learning to code when my husband needed a website. It wasn't love at first site but as time goes by I'm excited to getting better at coding everyday.

How it started?

I was never interested in programming, not even thought about it. But one day my husband needed a website and I said, let me try. From that day with many ups and downs I'm learning to code every free time I have.

How I stay motivated?

What makes me learn? The awerness that one day I'll be able to finally work the way I am and do what I like. Even if I'll never be pro in it. I like to learn, to evolve and this is what keeps me learning.

What to start with?

The best way is to learn from some kind of bootcamps, where you have your learning step by step. It's important not to only binge watch everything like a seasons on Netflix ;) You must practice a lot.
